Welcome to my corner of the internet. This is the first post on a site I have been meaning to build for a long time. After years of scattering notes across private repos, random Notion pages, and half-finished drafts in various markdown editors, I finally decided to consolidate everything into a proper portfolio and blog. The site is built with Hugo and the Blowfish theme, deployed via GitHub Pages, and supports both Chinese and English so I can write in whichever language fits the topic.

So what will you find here? Mostly technical writing about the projects I am working on -- deep dives into architecture decisions, post-mortems on things that broke in production, tutorials distilled from problems I spent too long debugging. I also plan to share design work, multimedia experiments, and the occasional essay on topics outside of engineering. Think of it as a living notebook rather than a polished publication.

If any of that sounds interesting, consider bookmarking the site or subscribing to the RSS feed. I am aiming to publish at least twice a month, though knowing myself, some months will be quieter than others. Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you find something useful.
